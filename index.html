About Our Mission

Teach , preserve and nurture the environment around us while we create a group of young leaders who will carry our vision of saving the environment

We Believe in Creating Sustainable Change

Founded in 2020, Waste management Foundation has grown from a small community initiative nation wide organization with projects aimed at being implemented in South Africa .Our approach focuses on empowering communities to become self-sufficient through Waste management education and economic development programs.

We work directly with local communities to teach waste management and develop solutions that respect cultural traditions while creating pathways to a brighter future. Our programs are designed to create lasting change, not temporary relief.

At Waste Management Foundation, We believe that every person deserves access to waste management education regardless of their circumstances and background . Our team of dedicated professionals and volunteers work tirelessly to turn this belief into reality.
